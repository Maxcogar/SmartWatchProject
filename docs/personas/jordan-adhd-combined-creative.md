## ADHD Presentation & Clinical Profile

### Core ADHD-C Symptoms (DSM-5 Based)
- **Inattentive Symptoms:** Difficulty sustaining attention on client work, frequently loses project files, forgets client deadlines
- **Hyperactive Symptoms:** Fidgets during client calls, difficulty sitting through long design reviews, restless energy
- **Impulsive Symptoms:** Interrupts client feedback sessions, makes quick design decisions without full consideration
- **Executive Function Challenges:** Struggles with project management, time estimation, and invoice organization
- **Emotional Dysregulation:** Rejection sensitivity with client feedback, mood fluctuations with project stress

### Strengths (Neurological Advantages)
- **Creative Hyperfocus:** Can produce exceptional design work during 4-8 hour creative flow states
- **Innovative Problem Solving:** Connects disparate concepts to create unique design solutions
- **Crisis Creativity:** Performs best under tight deadlines, produces breakthrough work under pressure
- **Empathetic Design:** Natural ability to understand diverse user needs and emotional responses
- **Pattern Recognition:** Excellent at spotting design trends and user experience patterns
- **Multi-Modal Thinking:** Seamlessly blends visual, auditory, and kinesthetic design approaches

### Daily Cognitive & Energy Profile
- **Peak Creative Hours:** Highly variable - could be 6 AM, 2 PM, or 10 PM depending on day
- **Task Switching Cost:** Extremely high - needs 15-30 minutes to refocus after interruption
- **Stimulation Sweet Spot:** Requires moderate background noise (coffee shop level) for optimal focus
- **Cognitive Load Threshold:** Can handle 3-4 active projects max before overwhelm sets in

**Success Metric:** Captures and successfully integrates 60% more inspiration moments into actual project work
